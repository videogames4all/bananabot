6 shot mulligans per player (re-roll any dice but the blue dice)
1 hole mulligan
1 styme
No mulligans if stymied.
Styme if double bogey, pass to someone. Styme is available after stymied rolls. No re-styming, no styming while styme is already out.
Stroke legend:
Fairway = 1 stroke
Green = 1 stroke
Rough = 2 strokes
Chip = 2 strokes
Bunker = 2 strokes
Hazard = 3 strokes
Green die is the putter, face value strokes
Sun subtracts 1 stroke
Wind adds 1 stroke
On orange die, roll a green, 1 stroke, ignore white die
On white die, roll a tap in, 2 strokes, ignore green/putter die
If both orange green and white tap in, ignore white tap in
